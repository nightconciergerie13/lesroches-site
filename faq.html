Everything you need to know before your stay at Les Roches. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, reach out directly — we reply within 24 hours.

Booking & Rates
How do I book a villa?+
You can book directly through our contact page or by writing to us on WhatsApp. Once we confirm availability and agree on the dates, we'll send you a formal booking confirmation. Direct bookings always receive our best rates — no platform fees.
What is the minimum stay?+
Our standard minimum stay is 7 nights. During peak season (December 15 – January 10 and Semana Santa) a minimum of 10 nights applies. Shorter stays may occasionally be available last-minute — just ask.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept bank transfers (EUR or USD), and payments via Wise or PayPal. A 40% deposit is required at booking to confirm your reservation. The remaining balance is due 60 days before arrival. For last-minute bookings (within 60 days), full payment is required at time of booking.
Are prices per night or per week?+
Rates are shown per night and vary by villa, season, and length of stay. Longer stays and direct bookings often qualify for preferential rates. Weekly and monthly rates are available — please contact us for a personalised quote.
Can I book multiple villas for a group or family?+
Absolutely. Les Roches was designed with groups and families in mind. You can book 2 to 6 villas simultaneously for a shared retreat while maintaining complete privacy in each villa. We offer special group rates and can arrange collective experiences (private dinners, surf lessons, yoga sessions) for the whole group. Contact us for a tailored proposal.
What is your cancellation policy?+
Cancellations made 90+ days before arrival: full deposit refund minus a €150 administrative fee.

Cancellations 60–89 days before arrival: 50% of total stay refunded.

Cancellations less than 60 days before arrival: no refund, though we'll do our best to find a new guest and offer you a credit for a future stay.

We strongly recommend travel insurance. Full details in our Booking Conditions.
Arrival & Check-In
What are the check-in and check-out times?+
Check-in is from 3:00 PM. Check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can often be arranged depending on availability — just let us know in advance. We can also organise luggage storage if you have a gap between checkout and your flight.
How do I get to Les Roches from San José?+
The most convenient option is a direct flight from San José (SJO) to Tambor airport (TMU) — a 30-minute flight with SANSA or Green Airways, followed by a 30-minute transfer to Santa Teresa. Alternatively, the scenic overland route takes around 4–5 hours via Puntarenas and the Paquera ferry (recommended for those who want to see the coast). We can arrange private transfers from any airport — ask us when booking.
Is there a concierge or welcome team on arrival?+
Yes. A member of our team will greet you on arrival, walk you through the villa, and ensure everything is perfect before leaving you to settle in. We'll share our contact number and are always available by WhatsApp during your stay for anything you need — from restaurant recommendations to a last-minute surf lesson booking.
Do I need a 4WD vehicle in Santa Teresa?+
During the dry season (December–April) a standard car is generally fine. In the rainy season (May–November) some roads in Santa Teresa can become rutted — a 4WD is recommended if you plan to explore beyond the main road. Rental quad bikes and ATVs are also available locally and are a popular way to get around.
Villas & Amenities
Are the pools private per villa?+
Yes. Every villa at Les Roches has its own private infinity pool — you won't share it with any other guests. Pools are heated to a comfortable temperature and maintained daily by our team.
Is there air conditioning?+
All bedrooms are equipped with air conditioning. The open-plan living areas are designed to capture tropical breezes naturally, creating comfortable airflow without AC. Ceiling fans are provided throughout.
Is there WiFi?+
Yes, high-speed fibre WiFi is available in all villas. That said, we designed Les Roches as a place to truly disconnect — the jungle, the pool, and the Pacific tend to be far more appealing than any screen.
Is there a kitchen? Are meals included?+
Each villa has a fully equipped kitchen with everything you need to cook. We stock your fridge with a welcome basket of fresh local produce before arrival. Meals are not included in the villa rate, but we can arrange a private chef for in-villa dining, or recommend our favourite local restaurants in Santa Teresa — some of the best in Costa Rica.
Is cleaning included?+
Daily housekeeping is included in all villa rates. Our team visits each morning (at a time agreed with you) to clean, refresh towels and linens, and maintain the villa and pool. Towels and premium bathroom amenities are provided and replenished throughout your stay.
Are the villas suitable for children?+
Yes, families are very welcome. Please note that all villas have private infinity pools — we recommend close supervision for young children at all times near the pool. Pool safety barriers can be arranged on request. We can provide travel cots, high chairs and baby equipment with advance notice.
Experiences & Santa Teresa
Can you help arrange surf lessons, yoga or other experiences?+
Absolutely. We work with the best local instructors and operators to arrange everything from surf lessons at Playa Carmen, private yoga sessions at your villa, horseback riding on the beach, waterfall hikes, sport fishing, snorkelling, ATV tours, and more. Just let us know your interests and we'll have it all organised before you arrive. See our Experiences page for inspiration.
What is the best time of year to visit Santa Teresa?+
Santa Teresa is a year-round destination. The dry season (December – April) offers reliably sunny days, ideal for beaches and outdoor activities. The green season (May – November) brings lush jungle, fewer crowds, lower rates, and dramatic afternoon storms that pass quickly — many guests find it even more atmospheric. The sea and surf are excellent year-round.
How far is the beach from Les Roches?+
Les Roches is located about 5 minutes by car (or 15 minutes on foot) from Playa Santa Teresa — one of the most beautiful surf beaches in Central America. We provide beach towels and beach bags in each villa. Quad bike rentals are available locally for easy, fun beach access.
Are there good restaurants nearby?+
Santa Teresa has a surprisingly excellent food scene — fresh ceviche, whole-fish grills, organic cafés, and inventive fusion restaurants. We have a curated list of our favourites (updated regularly) that we share with every guest on arrival. We can also make reservations on your behalf.
Is Santa Teresa safe?+
Santa Teresa is one of the safest and most welcoming areas of Costa Rica — predominantly a community of surfers, wellness seekers, and international expats. As with any destination, we recommend standard precautions: not leaving valuables on the beach, using the villa safe for passports and cash. Les Roches has secure gated access and 24h on-call support.
