The story behind Les Roches

The Vision

Les Roches was born from a shared obsession: to build the retreat we always dreamed of but could never find. After years of traveling through Costa Rica, Ian, Marie and Martin chose Santa Teresa — and designed six private villas that are both rigorously minimalist and deeply human.

Ian

Co-Founder & Operations

A seasoned hospitality veteran with 15 years in luxury resort management across Central America. Ian brings precision and warmth to every guest interaction.

Marie

Co-Founder & Design

An architect and designer obsessed with simplicity and natural materials. Marie crafted every villa to feel like home, only better.

Martin

Co-Founder & Guest Experience

A serial entrepreneur with deep roots in Costa Rica. Martin's vision is to create meaningful connections between guests and the pura vida lifestyle.

Six houses. One philosophy. Nothing between you and the beauty of Costa Rica.

🔒

Privacy

Your villa is yours alone. Complete solitude, whenever you want it. No shared spaces, no schedules.

🌴

Nature

The jungle is not a backdrop. It is the experience. Howler monkeys, sunset, ocean breezes—daily miracles.

✨

Simplicity

We removed everything that wasn't essential. Minimalist design meets maximum comfort.
