The Villa

The largest villa
for families or friends who travel together

Villa Onyx is our flagship property — a spacious three-bedroom masterpiece set amid gardens and overlooking the pool courtyard. With room for six guests and plenty of shared living space, it's perfect for families, friend groups, or anyone who needs multiple bedrooms without compromising on luxury.

Each bedroom is thoughtfully designed with its own personality and charm. The expansive pool area becomes the heart of the villa, surrounded by lounging spaces and dining areas. Perfect for making memories with your travel companions while enjoying complete privacy from the outside world.
