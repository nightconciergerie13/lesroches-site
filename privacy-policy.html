Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This policy is also available in French and Spanish.

1. Who we are

Les Roches Retreat operates the website lesrochesretreat.com and manages six private luxury villas in Santa Teresa, Costa Rica. References to "we", "us" or "our" in this policy refer to Les Roches Retreat.

Contact: hello@lesrochesretreat.com — Calle Vista De Olas, Santa Teresa, Puntarenas, Costa Rica.

2. What data we collect

We collect personal data only when you voluntarily provide it. This includes:

  • Booking requests: your name, email address, phone number, desired dates and number of guests.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: your email address.
  • Contact messages: your name, email address and the content of your message.
  • Visitor analytics: page visited, approximate country of origin, device type, and referral source — collected anonymously via our own analytics system (no third-party cookies).

We do not collect sensitive personal data (health information, financial data, identity documents) through this website.

3. How we use your data

  • Booking requests: to respond to your enquiry, confirm availability and finalize your reservation via WhatsApp and email.
  • Newsletter: to send occasional updates on availability, special offers and news about Les Roches. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Analytics: to understand how visitors use our website and improve our content.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): processing necessary to handle your booking request.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): anonymous analytics to improve our service.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): newsletter subscriptions. You may withdraw consent at any time.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure hosted in the EU). Booking request emails are processed via EmailJS, which transmits your data by email only and does not store it.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction.

6. Data retention

  • Booking enquiries: retained for 3 years after your last interaction with us.
  • Newsletter emails: retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Analytics data: retained for 13 months on a rolling basis.

7. Cookies

We use only strictly necessary cookies and our own first-party analytics. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. A cookie consent banner is displayed on your first visit and you may change your preferences at any time.

8. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@lesrochesretreat.com. We will respond within 30 days.

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority (e.g. CNIL in France, AEPD in Spain, ICO in the UK).

9. Third-party services

Our website uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts — font delivery (Google LLC, USA)
  • EmailJS — email transmission for booking requests
  • Supabase — secure data storage
  • Flatpickr — date picker (loaded locally, no external requests)

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date of the last update is shown at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:
