The Retreat

Six villas,
one wild escape.

Perched above the jungle, five minutes from the heart of Santa Teresa — and a world away from its noise. Les Roches is a private sanctuary conceived for those who seek space, silence, and raw beauty.

Six minimalist villas, each opening onto a private infinity pool suspended above the jungle canopy and the Pacific horizon. No compromise. No distraction. Just the sound of the ocean, the birds, and the wind through the trees.
