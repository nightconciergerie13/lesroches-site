Santa Teresa

The most beautiful village in Costa Rica

About the destination

Santa Teresa is the surf capital of Costa Rica, nestled on the Nicoya Peninsula where the Pacific crashes against pristine beaches. Known worldwide for legendary beach breaks and stunning sunsets, it's a place where bohemian spirit meets natural splendor. Here, howler monkeys welcome your mornings, local surfers share tips in beach cafés, and the pura vida lifestyle isn't a slogan—it's a way of being. Whether you're seeking solitude or connection, adventure or rest, Santa Teresa delivers pura vida at its finest.

Getting There

✈️

Fly to San José (SJO)

Then take a domestic flight to Tambor or drive 3 hours 30 minutes. Scenic route through Costa Rica's heartland.

✈️

Fly to Liberia (LIR)

Direct flights from US and Europe. Then drive 2 hours 30 minutes to Santa Teresa. The closer option.

🚗

Private Transfers

Les Roches arranges direct transfers from the airport. Let us handle the logistics.

Best Time to Visit

November – April (Dry Season)

The best season. Sunny, warm, and perfect for surf. Days are crystal clear, nights cool and comfortable. This is peak season—book ahead.

May – October (Rainy Season)

Warm, lush, and atmospheric. The jungle is at its greenest. Fewer crowds, lower rates. Afternoon showers cool things down. Average temperature stays around 28°C year-round—it's always warm in Costa Rica.
